# Engela Art Website: Product Overview

## Purpose of the Website

The Engela Art website will be a professional online portfolio and sales-inquiry website for original paintings.

The website has three main goals:

1. Present the artist and her work in a trustworthy and personal way.
2. Help visitors browse and understand the paintings.
3. Make it easy for interested buyers to contact the artist formally.

The website will not be a normal online shop. Visitors will not place paintings in a cart or pay directly on the website. Original paintings are unique, and availability, shipping, pickup, and payment often need a personal conversation first.

## The Brand and Visual Style

The public brand name will be **Engela Art**.

The website will have a light, warm, gallery-like design. Its colors will be inspired by the artist's autumn theme:

- Warm cream and sand backgrounds
- Brown and charcoal text
- Terracotta, orange, peach, burgundy, and ochre accents
- Small amounts of blue-green for contrast

The website should feel warm, artistic, honest, and professional without competing with the paintings.

The current Engela Art logo, banners, autumn pattern, and portrait will be prepared for different uses:

- A simple logo or wordmark in the header
- A larger logo in the footer or About page
- A small icon for browser tabs and bookmarks
- A social-media profile image
- A branded preview image when the website is shared
- A professional portrait on the About page

## Languages

The complete website will be available in:

- Norwegian
- English

Norwegian will be the original source for the written content. The artist and developer will agree on the Norwegian wording together. The developer will then prepare and approve the English version.

Visitors can change language at any time. The website will remember their chosen language.

## Main Pages

### Home

The Home page will create the first impression.

It will include:

- Engela Art branding
- One strong seasonal painting
- A short introduction to the artist
- A hand-picked selection of paintings
- A clear link to browse all paintings
- A secondary link to commission information
- A short introduction to the artist's story
- Testimonials later, when genuine reviews exist

### Paintings

The Paintings page will show all public paintings in a clear grid.

Visitors can:

- View available, reserved, and sold paintings
- Filter by status
- Filter by landscape, portrait, or square orientation
- Combine filters
- Sort by newest, oldest, size, or price
- Share a filtered page with another person

Each painting card will show:

- Image
- Official title
- Status
- Medium or dimensions
- Price in NOK

Sold paintings will remain visible as part of the artist's portfolio.

### Painting Details

Each painting will have its own page with:

- Large images
- Fullscreen image viewing
- Official title
- Painting reference number
- Medium and materials
- Width, height, and depth
- Creation year
- Price
- Availability status
- Description
- Optional note from the artist
- Care instructions
- Shipping and framing information
- The correct inquiry button for its status

Available paintings will invite a purchase inquiry.

Reserved paintings will allow visitors to join an interest list.

Sold paintings will allow visitors to ask about similar work.

### Commissions

The Commissions page will explain how a buyer can ask about a new painting inspired by the artist's existing work.

The artist does not promise:

- An exact copy
- A painting in an unrelated style
- Automatic acceptance of every request

The page will explain:

- Starting prices by size
- Approximate timelines
- How the artist reviews the idea
- The written proposal
- The 50% deposit and 50% final-payment plan
- The concept confirmation
- One progress update
- How major changes are handled
- Delivery and portfolio-use expectations

### About

The About page will focus on the person behind the paintings.

It will include:

- A professional portrait
- A personal story
- Why she paints
- Her inspiration
- Her materials and process
- Relevant background
- Contact and social-media links
- Testimonials after genuine reviews exist

The writing should feel genuine and specific, not like generic advertising.

### Contact

The Contact page will contain one main form for:

- General questions
- Questions about an available painting
- Joining an interest list
- Asking about similar work
- Commission inquiries

The form will request:

- Name
- Email
- Optional phone number
- Message

The form will not request a postal address.

After submission:

- The artist receives an email.
- The visitor normally receives an automatic acknowledgement.
- The website explains that the inquiry is not a reservation.
- The artist aims to reply personally within two business days.

### Privacy

The Privacy page will explain:

- What personal information is collected
- Why it is needed
- Which services process it
- How long it is kept
- The visitor's rights
- The language-preference cookie

### Sales and Returns

This page will explain:

- Paintings are sold unframed.
- Listed prices exclude shipping.
- Pickup can be arranged in Nannestad.
- The home address is shared privately, not published.
- Shipping is tracked and appropriately insured where possible.
- International shipping is considered individually.
- Payment is handled through an invoice.
- Withdrawal, complaints, damage, returns, and photographs are handled according to approved terms and Norwegian law.

It will also include frequently asked questions.

## Painting Images and Watermark

The artist's private original image files will remain unwatermarked and stored outside the public website.

Every painting image published on the website will receive a subtle Engela Art watermark. This includes:

- Home page painting images
- Gallery thumbnails
- Painting detail images
- Fullscreen images
- Room-context images
- Painting-specific social preview images

The watermark will:

- Use the text `© Engela Art`
- Identify Engela Art after casual copying
- Use a consistent size and placement
- Remain visible without covering important artwork details
- Be added directly to the public image file

The portrait, logo, and normal brand graphics will not receive the painting watermark.

## Prices and Availability

Every painting will show a public price in NOK.

Sold paintings will keep their historical listed price so visitors can understand the artist's price level.

The website will use three statuses:

- **Available:** Visitors can inquire about buying it.
- **Reserved:** Another buyer currently has priority, but others can join the interest list.
- **Sold:** Full payment has been received.

Submitting an inquiry does not automatically reserve a painting.

Availability is always confirmed personally by the artist.

## Payment and Delivery

After the artist confirms the painting, buyer, and delivery arrangement, the buyer receives an invoice.

Payment alternatives can include:

- Bank transfer
- Vipps Business
- PayPal Business

Bank transfer will be the preferred option because it normally has the lowest cost.

The website itself will not collect payment.

For shipped paintings:

- Payment must normally be received before dispatch.
- Shipping must be tracked.
- Suitable insurance or declared-value coverage should be checked.
- The buyer receives tracking information by email.

## Certificate of Authenticity

Every sold original painting will receive:

- A signed physical certificate
- A matching PDF certificate by email

The certificate will include:

- Engela Art and artist information
- Certificate number
- Painting reference number
- Title
- Year
- Medium
- Dimensions
- Image
- Signature
- Sale date

## Reviews and Testimonials

The website will be ready to display testimonials, but it will not show an empty review section.

Reviews should be:

- Genuine
- Requested neutrally
- Published with the correct permission
- Linked to the original Google review when applicable

Testimonials can later appear on the Home and About pages.

## Accessibility and Quality

The website will be designed to work well for:

- Mobile phones
- Tablets
- Desktop computers
- Keyboard users
- Screen-reader users
- Visitors who prefer reduced motion

The main goal is a smooth, calm, fast, and accessible experience.

## What Is Not Included in the First Version

The first version will not include:

- Shopping cart
- Online checkout
- Customer accounts
- Admin dashboard
- Database
- Newsletter
- Embedded social-media feed
- Automatic invoicing
- Automatic shipping calculation
- Customer tracking portal

These can be considered later if real experience shows they are needed.

## What Must Be Ready Before Launch

Before public launch, the project needs:

- At least six complete paintings
- At least three featured paintings
- Approved images and watermarks
- Norwegian and English content
- Final artist biography
- ENK and business details
- Domain and business mailbox
- Reviewed privacy and sales terms
- Working inquiry emails
- Tested mobile and desktop layouts
- Accessibility and performance checks
