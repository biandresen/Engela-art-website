# Legal Information Structure

## Decision

V1 keeps two canonical localized legal and buyer-guidance pages:

- `/no/personvern` and `/en/privacy`
- `/no/salg-og-retur` and `/en/sales-and-returns`

The site does not add separate Terms and Payment/Delivery routes for v1.
Instead, the Sales/Returns page contains the visitor-facing terms, payment,
pickup, shipping, withdrawal, complaint, and return guidance.

## Why

Engela Art is an inquiry-first portfolio and sales site, not a webshop. There is
no cart, checkout, customer account, database-backed order flow, or payment
provider integration in v1. Splitting the legal information into several small
pages would make the footer and sitemap more complex without adding meaningful
clarity for buyers.

Keeping the structure to two pages gives visitors a simple choice:

- Privacy explains how inquiry, email, analytics, monitoring, and language-cookie
  data is handled.
- Sales and returns explains seller identity, terms, manual payment, pickup,
  shipping, withdrawal, complaints, returns, copyright, and contact routes.

If the site later adds checkout, digital products, prints, subscriptions, or a
larger operational sales flow, the legal structure should be reviewed again and
may be split into separate Terms and Payment/Delivery pages.
